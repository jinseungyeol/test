ALBUM INTRODUCTION

Peaches

Justin Bieber

March 19, 2021

"Peaches" is a song recorded by Canadian singer Justin Bieber, featuring vocals from fellow Canadian singer-songwriter Daniel Caesar and American singer-songwriter Giveon. It was released on March 19, 2021, by Def Jam as the fifth single from Bieber's sixth studio album, Justice. Bieber, Caesar and Giveon co-wrote the song alongside Andrew Watt, Louis Bell, Luis Martinez Jr. and Bernard "Harv" Harvey, while the latter produced the song alongside Shndo. The official remix features vocals from another fellow American singer-songwriter Usher and American rappers Ludacris and Snoop Dogg.

Often referred to as the "Prince of Pop", his versatile cross-genre approach towards music and showmanship have received critical acclaim and recognition.
